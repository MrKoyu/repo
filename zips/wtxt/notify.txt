[COLOR gold]HOW TO SET-UP INFUSION 2[/COLOR]

[B][COLOR deepskyblue]1 •  [/COLOR][/B] [COLOR white]Enter in APPS main menu then long press on TMDB Helper add-on/Settings/Accounts/Authenticate Trakt account...[/COLOR]

[B][COLOR deepskyblue]2 •  [/COLOR][/B] [COLOR white]Go to SYSTEM main menu then press down for submenu and enter in RESOLVE URL RD and authorise your Real-Debrid. If you have a different Debrid service other than RD then enter in UNIVERSAL URL which can be found on the same SYSTEM submenu.[/COLOR]

[B][COLOR deepskyblue]3 •  [/COLOR][/B] [COLOR white]Enter in APPS main menu then long press on SEREN add-on/Settings/Accounts/Enable Real-Debrid then Authorise Real-Debrid.[/COLOR]

[B][COLOR deepskyblue]4 •  [/COLOR][/B] [COLOR white]Enter in APPS main menu then long press on Shadow add-on/Settings/Debrid/Debrid Service/Re auth. RD[/COLOR]

[B][COLOR deepskyblue]5 •  [/COLOR][/B] [COLOR white]Go to SYSTEM main menu then press down for submenu and enter in INSTALL - ZIP then My add-ons/VideoPlayer InputStream and enable InputStream Adaptive[/COLOR]

[B][COLOR deepskyblue]6 •  [/COLOR][/B] [COLOR white]Click on InputStream Adaptive then configure. Set Max. Resolution general decoder to 1080p. Set secure decoder to 1080p as well then press OK to save changes.[/COLOR]

[B][COLOR deepskyblue]7 •  [/COLOR][/B] [COLOR white]Enter in APPS main menu then long press on YouTube add-on/Settings/General/Video Quality to 1080p then scroll to MPEG-DASH/enable Use MPEG-DASH and set Video Quality to 1080p. This step will get you 1080p YouTube videos when using some of NuMb3r5 categories.[/COLOR]
